About Us

Our Company started in 2025 with the mission to recycle plastic waste into usable resin. We operate a grinding and pelletizing line and serve manufacturers, packaging companies and artisans.

Our Values

  • Quality first

    • At Re-Earth Solution, we specialize in manufacturing premium-quality granules for industrial and creative applications. Our products are engineered to deliver consistent performance, durability, and ease of use.

  • Environmental responsibility

    • At Re-Earth Solution, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality granules for industrial and creative applications, while keeping sustainability at the core of our operations. Our products are designed to deliver consistent performance, durability, and ease of use, all while minimizing environmental impact.

  • Marketing-Oriented

    • At Re-Earth Solution, we turn high-quality granules into endless possibilities. From molding to casting, our granules provide consistent quality, strength, and reliability, making them the preferred choice for professionals and creators.
